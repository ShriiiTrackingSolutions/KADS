"Designing Homes That Tell Your Story"

Kunal Agarwal Design Studio

KADS is a creative interior design studio dedicated to transforming spaces with elegance, functionality, and style. We specialize in personalized solutions, blending modern aesthetics with client vision to create inspiring environments. From concept to completion, KADS delivers excellence in every detail—whether it’s home interiors, office makeovers, or 3D visualizations. Your dream space starts with us.

With a passion for design and a commitment to quality, KADS offers a full range of services including space planning, consultations, and realistic 3D visualizations. Our team works closely with clients to understand their needs and lifestyle, ensuring each project reflects their personality. At KADS, we don’t just design spaces—we craft experiences that feel like home.
