Kunal Agarwal Design Studio

Kunal Agrawal Design Studio is an architecture and design practice dedicated to crafting built environments that are context-sensitive, functionally efficient, and aesthetically timeless. Founded by architect Kunal Agrawal, the studio operates at the intersection of innovation and practicality, with a strong belief that architecture must respond intelligently to its surroundings, users, and climate.

Our work spans across residential, commercial, and institutional projects, each characterized by a meticulous attention to detail, material honesty, and spatial clarity. We believe architecture is more than form-making—it is about shaping experiences, solving real-world problems, and enhancing the way people live and interact with space.

Every project at Kunal Agrawal Design Studio begins with a deep exploration of site, program, and client aspirations. This is followed by a rigorous design process that integrates architecture, interior design, and landscape to deliver cohesive, sustainable, and enduring solutions.

Whether it’s a private residence, a workspace, or a public building, our aim is to create architecture that is both contemporary and rooted in purpose—architecture that stands the test of time.

Our expertise

At Kunal Agrawal Design Studio, we welcome opportunities to collaborate on projects that seek thoughtful design, spatial efficiency, and architectural integrity. Whether you are planning a new build, adaptive reuse, or a spatial retrofit, we invite you to share your vision with us.

Our expertise spans across:

  • Architectural Design – Concept development to construction documentation
  • Interior Architecture – Spatial planning, material specification, and detailing
  • Urban & Site Planning – Context-driven layouts and landscape integration
  • 3D Visualization – Concept renders to working model integration
  • Design-Build & Turnkey Solutions – End-to-end project execution
  • Renovation & Adaptive Reuse – Reimagining spaces for contemporary use
