About Us

Crafting Timeless Spaces

Kunal Agarwal Design Studio, widely recognized as KADS, began its journey with a passion for transforming interiors into immersive experiences. Over the years, KADS has grown from a boutique interior design studio into a comprehensive design house, offering curated solutions across interiors, architecture, and space planning. Today, KADS stands as a symbol of creativity, elegance, and thoughtful design, earning its place among the distinguished design firms in India.

Why KADS

At KADS, we blend precision with purpose. For us, design is not just about how things look—it’s about how they work, how they feel, and how they last.

Innovative Design Thinking

KADS blends creativity with strategic insight, delivering spaces that are not just beautiful but meaningful. Our design approach challenges the ordinary, ensuring every project is as unique as our client’s vision.

End-to-End Project Expertise

From the initial concept to final execution, KADS offers a seamless design experience. Our in-house team ensures consistent quality, timely delivery, and a stress-free journey through each phase of your interior transformation.

Client-Centric Philosophy

At KADS, your needs shape our process. We pride ourselves on being great listeners and collaborative partners, translating your lifestyle and aspirations into functional, luxurious interiors that feel truly personal.
